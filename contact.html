Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer with JOPA Foundation?

+

You can volunteer by filling out our contact form with "I want to volunteer" as the subject. We'll respond with available opportunities and next steps. No experience is necessary!

How can I make a donation?

+

You can donate through our website or contact us directly for bank transfer details. All donations are securely processed and used to support our programs.

What is your organization's registration status?

+

JOPA Foundation Uganda is currently undergoing registration. We are committed to full transparency and compliance with all regulatory requirements.

Can organizations partner with JOPA Foundation?

+

Yes! We welcome partnerships with NGOs, government agencies, and private sector organizations. Contact us to discuss collaboration opportunities.
