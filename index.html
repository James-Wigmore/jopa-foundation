Who We Are

JOPA Foundation Uganda is a community-based organization committed to empowering individuals and communities through education, innovation, strategic partnerships, science, technology, and sustainable development. We work alongside communities to create opportunities that improve livelihoods, strengthen resilience, and inspire lasting socio-economic transformation across Uganda.

Our Strategic Areas

We focus on eight key areas to create lasting positive change

🎓

Education & Training

Providing quality education and skills training to build a knowledgeable and capable workforce.

👩‍💼

Youth & Women Empowerment

Equipping young people and women with skills, resources, and opportunities for success.

🌾

Agriculture & Food Security

Promoting sustainable farming practices and ensuring food security for rural communities.

🔬

Science, Technology & Innovation

Fostering innovation and technology adoption to drive socio-economic development.

🏥

Community Health & Welfare

Enhancing access to quality healthcare and improving overall community health outcomes.

🌍

Environmental Sustainability

Promoting environmental conservation and sustainable resource management practices.

💼

Entrepreneurship & Economic Empowerment

Supporting small businesses with trainings on business management & financial literacy, and creating income-generating opportunities for communities.

🔍

Research & Development

Promoting innovation through research, data-driven insights, and collaborative projects that address community challenges, inform decision-making, and support sustainable development.

💻

Digital Literacy & ICT Skills

Equipping individuals with practical computer and digital skills for education, employment, entrepreneurship, and active participation in the digital economy..
