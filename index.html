Who We Are

JOPA Foundation Uganda is a community-based organization committed to creating sustainable opportunities and improving livelihoods through innovation, education, and empowerment.

We work to advance sustainable development across nine key strategic focus areas, guided by principles of integrity, community empowerment, and innovation.

Our Strategic Focus Areas

We focus on nine key areas to create lasting positive change

🎓

Education & Training

Providing quality education and skills training to build a knowledgeable and capable workforce.

👩‍💼

Youth & Women Empowerment

Equipping young people and women with skills, resources, and opportunities for success.

🌾

Agriculture & Food Security

Promoting sustainable farming practices and ensuring food security for rural communities.

🔬

Science, Technology & Innovation

Fostering innovation and technology adoption to drive socio-economic development.

🏥

Community Health & Welfare

Enhancing access to quality healthcare and improving overall community health outcomes.

🌍

Environmental Sustainability

Promoting environmental conservation and sustainable resource management practices.

💼

Entrepreneurship & Economic Empowerment

Supporting small businesses and creating income-generating opportunities for communities.

🔍

Research & Development

Conducting research to inform evidence-based programming and policy advocacy.

💻

Digital Literacy & ICT Skills

Building digital competencies to enable participation in the knowledge economy.
